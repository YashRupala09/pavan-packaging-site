Our Story

Founded by Maheshbhai Dudhat in Ahmedabad, Pavan Packaging started with a mission to solve breakage issues in fragile shipments. We innovated with inflatable air column bags that are superior to foam and bubble wrap.

With over 5 years of manufacturing and packaging experience, we serve hundreds of clients across industries including electronics, glassware, cosmetics, and healthcare.
