How Air Column Bags Are Made

Step 1: Polyethylene Film Layering

We begin with high-quality polyethylene (PE) and nylon films that form the base material. These films provide the strength and flexibility needed for shock protection.

Step 2: Heat Sealing of Air Columns

The film is passed through a sealing machine where vertical lines are sealed to create independent air columns. These columns are what protect your items from damage.

Step 3: Air Valve & Inflation Testing

Each column is fitted with a one-way air valve and tested for smooth inflation. This ensures every chamber inflates independently for maximum protection.

Step 4: Cutting & Ready to Use

The sheets are cut into required sizes, flattened, and packed. When used, they’re inflated in seconds and ready to secure fragile items for shipping or storage.
